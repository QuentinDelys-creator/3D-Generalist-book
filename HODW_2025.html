House Of Dancing Water is a major project in the field of mega-events, a show created by Franco Dragone and staged at City Of Dreams in Macau for Melco Resorts & Entertainment. Having had the opportunity to be part of this project as a 3D Generalist, I participated in the creation of 3D assets and some animations for the main stage and the lobby on a huge LED screen. Part of the production was carried out in Belgium and the last month on site in Macau, China. All this was done with the video/audiovisual production company Drop The Spoon Srl.
